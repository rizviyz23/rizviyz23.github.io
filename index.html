The Fun Physics behind Roller Coasters!

Roller coasters use basic ideas from physics and engineering to create fast, exciting, and carefully controlled motion. In this post, I break down how concepts like energy, speed, acceleration, and forces work together to produce the twists, turns, and drops we experience on a ride. By understanding these principles, we will see how real-world physics shapes the design and safety of every roller coaster. If you enjoy these rides, this one is for you!

But for those who don't...I hope that after you give this a read, you grow a fonder appreciation for the science. It may still be intimidating or even anxiety-inducing for you, but this blog will have you see these coasters differently- with ample respect behind the amount of thought and expertise in their fun-filled safety design. Hey, maybe this blog is enough to convince you to face your fear!

The Beauty of Slow Shutter - Motion Blur and Slow Shutter Techniques using Dazz Cam!

The Beauty of Slow Shutter is exactly what this is about. I want you to appreciate the beauty behind every intentional move behind videography, starting with slow shutter. This is for beginners- for those who want to get used to photography on portable apps on their phone! I demonstrate the most essential part of photography and videography- motion blur. My tutorial uses slow shutter on Dazz Cam to create this effect, taking you on a straight-to-the point, captivating venture.

Well..... why Dazz Cam anyways? It is free, versatile, flexible, and an extremely high-quality photography app, and many users have timelessly conveyed their appreciation for it. We will start with understanding simple buttons on Dazz Cam, adjusting ISO and speed levels, and getting a grasp on noise, exposure, and frames per second. In the end of my tutorial, we will also see some other super cool motion blur results!
